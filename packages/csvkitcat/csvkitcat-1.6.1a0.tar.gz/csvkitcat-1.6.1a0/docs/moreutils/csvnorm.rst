csvnorm
*******

.. contents:: :local:


Description
===========

Normalize whitespace, newlines, and character casing.




Examples
========


Basic examples::

    $ csvnorm examples/consec_ws.csv

    id,phrase
    1,hello world
    2,good bye
    3,a ok



    $ csvnorm examples/multi1.csv

    id,text
    1,hey
    2,hello world
    3,"to be, or not to be?"
